Base Condition Condition A disorder, abnormality, problem, injury, complaint, risk, functionality, concern, illness, disease, ailment, sickness, affliction, upset, difficulty, disorder, symptom, worry, or trouble. A ConditionPresent finding implies the existence of a condition as an entity. 'Condition' is interpreted broadly to cover states of existence - things the patient is or has.
Base Entry DomainResource Metadata attributes that apply to any item represented in the standard health record. An Entry may not belong exclusively to a single person's health record, but could represent an entity that surfaces in multiple records, such as organizations or practitioners. If the entry belongs to a single person's record, then the identity of the person of record must be recorded.
Base Observation Observation Base class for observations (measurements, tests, or questions) and a value containing the result (answer). The subject of a finding can be the entire patient, or an entity such as a location body structure, intervention, or condition. Things observed about the subject can include social and behavioral factors, subjective and objective observations, and assessments.
